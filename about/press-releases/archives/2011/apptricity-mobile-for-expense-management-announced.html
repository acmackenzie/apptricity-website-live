Apptricity Mobile for Expense Management Announced

DALLAS, May 10, 2011 – Apptricity Corporation, a pioneer and leader of Finance Management, Supply Chain Management and Workforce Management, announced today its planned release of Apptricity Mobile for Expense Management.

Apptricity Mobile for Expense Management is the complementary mobile app to Apptricity Expense Management, a leading solution for automated travel and expense report management used worldwide by mid- to large-size enterprises including several Fortune 10 customers.

“With more than 90% of Americans using mobile technology, it’s a natural evolution to move from expense reporting on the PC to the smartphone,” said Tim Garcia, Chief Executive Officer & President of Apptricity. “Apptricity Mobile for Expense has an easy user interface that allows employees and managers more flexibility in managing travel and expenses outside the office.”

Apptricity Mobile for Expense Management allows employees the ability to create expense reports on the go, capture and upload expenses to their smartphones or tablet, and view unassigned expenses to add to a report. Managers have the added ability to view submitted reports, and either approve/deny to continue the workflow process. From the mobile app, data is synced to the user application.

Apptricity Mobile for Expense Management supports Apple iPhone/iPad, Android, BlackBerry and Windows Mobile.
