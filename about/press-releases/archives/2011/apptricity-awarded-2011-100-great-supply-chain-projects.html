Apptricity Awarded 2011 “100 Great Supply Chain Projects"

DALLAS, May 4, 2011 – Apptricity Corporation, a pioneer and leader in Finance Management, Supply Chain Management and Workforce Management automated solutions, announced today that its Transportation Management suite of solutions has been awarded “100 Great Supply Chain Projects” for 2011 by Supply & Demand Chain Executive magazine. The publication’s award recognizes supply chain solution and service providers that assist their customers and clients achieve supply chain excellence.

“For more than five years we have focused on designing and deploying cutting-edge, supply chain logistics,” said Timothy D. Garcia, President & CEO of Apptricity Corporation. “We are exceptionally pleased with the technology we deliver to PD TIS in its support for the Department of Defense.”

Apptricity Transportation Management (TMS) suite of solutions includes Apptricity Asset Management, Apptricity Graphical Asset Management, Apptricity Fleet Management, and Apptricity Freight Invoice Management, and provides the military's Program Director Transportation Information Systems (PD TIS) with Theater Operations capabilities for Afghanistan, Iraq, Kuwait and Germany. Using the Apptricity TMS solution, Army Transportation Units and their command structure have real-time visibility and the capability to manage, plan, coordinate, and report cargo movement within theater.

"Our goal with this year's '100' is to put the spotlight on successful and innovative transformation projects," said Barry Hochfelder, editor of Supply & Demand Chain Executive. "The projects featured in the '100' article can serve as a roadmap for supply chain executives looking for new opportunities to drive improvement in their own operations."

About Apptricity

Founded in 1999, Apptricity Corporation provides the core enterprise applications and services used to automate financial management, advanced logistics, supply chain and workforce management. Customers benefit from reduced operational costs due to improvements in efficiencies and employee compliance. Apptricity also offers multiple deployment alternatives to address customer demands including a perpetual license as well as Software-as-a-Service (SaaS). Built on an advanced Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA) framework, Apptricity solutions provide platform independence, broad-reaching integration, mass customization and hassle-free migration.

About PD TIS

Program Director Transportation Information Systems (PD TIS) is a respected producer of transportation management Information Technology (IT) supporting the Department of Defense (DoD) digital logistics environment. The web address is http://www.tis.army.mil.
