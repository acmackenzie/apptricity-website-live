Apptricity Signs Task Order for PD TIS AMR

DALLAS, TEXAS, September 15, 2011 – Apptricity Corporation, an industry leader in automated Supply Chain, Logistics, Spend and Workforce Management commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) solutions, announced today that Apptricity Government Services has signed a new task order continuing support for the Product Director, Transportation Information Systems (PD TIS) Theater Operations Air Movement Request (AMR) efforts.

PD TIS supports the DoD's digital logistics environment through transportation and logistics management for cargo and personnel worldwide. Apptricity's Transportation Management solutions solve logistics problems by providing detailed data for operations, assets, schedules, cargo/personnel needs, availability and movement.

“We are pleased to extend our involvement with the PD TIS program office mission of providing the best transportation management solutions to the warfighter,” said Ralph McCoy, Executive Vice President of Government Services for Apptricity Corporation. “We look forward to continued success of the Theater Operations Air Movement initiative."

"Our relationship with PD TIS continues to expand with our software support for continuance in theater operations," added Timothy D. Garcia, CEO & President of Apptricity. "Our overall mission is to provide the best technology and solutions for both Government and Commercial enterprises. We look forward to our continued relationship with PD TIS in its broader support for the Department of Defense as it relates to Air Movement Operations."
