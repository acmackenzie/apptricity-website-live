Apptricity Signs New Task Order with DoD

DALLAS, August 23, 2011 – Apptricity Corporation, an industry leader in automated Supply Chain, Logistics, Spend and Workforce Management COTS solutions, announced today that its Government Services organization has signed a new task order continuing support for the Product Director, Transportation Information Systems (PD TIS) Theater Operations efforts.

PD TIS supports the DoD's digital logistics environment through transportation and logistics management for cargo and personnel worldwide. Apptricity's Transportation Management solutions solve logistics problems by providing detailed data for operations schedules as well as cargo/personnel needs, availability and movement.

“We are pleased to extend our involvement with the PD TIS program office mission of providing the best transportation management solutions to the warfighter,” said Ralph McCoy, Executive Vice President of Government Services for Apptricity Corporation. “We look forward to continued success of the Theater Operations Air Movement initiative and are proud of our government services team that supports this important capability."

Timothy D. Garcia, CEO & President of Apptricity Corporation, adds “Apptricity's overall mission is to provide leading edge solutions for both Government and Commercial enterprises. We look forward to our continued relationship with PD TIS in its broader support for the Department of Defense.”
