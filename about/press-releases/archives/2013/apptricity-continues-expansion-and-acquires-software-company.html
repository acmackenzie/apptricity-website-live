Apptricity Continues Expansion and Acquires Software Technology

July 08, 2013 – Dallas, Texas – Apptricity Corporation, provider of the Jetstream suite of supply chain, e-procurement and financial productivity solutions, announced today it has acquired a content management software that will augment Jetstream with robust document management functionality.

Continuing to leverage the cloud, customers can now add, delete, control versions and edit important reference documents or images relevant to their business. “We've never stood still at Apptricity,” said Tim Garcia, President and CEO of Apptricity Corporation. “This acquisition demonstrates that we are continuing to grow and invest in technology that brings value to our company. With input from our experienced advisory board and our development team, we determined this acquisition would provide customers functionality they need and give us a competitive edge. Our software already has many innovative features,” said Brad Jestis, Vice President of Software Development at Apptricity Corporation. “But now customers will have access to a robust set of document management functionality including drag and drop file upload, full text search, and on-demand editing.”
