General Datatech Selects Apptricity Jetstream to Automate Warehouse Operations

DALLAS, Jan. 30, 2013 – Apptricity, provider of the Jetstream suite of supply chain, e-procurement and financial productivity solutions, announced today that it has been selected by General Datatech, one of the world's largest Cisco resellers, to automate warehouse management operations.

General Datatech's chief executive officer, J.W. Roberts, said his company found Apptricity Jetstream, which empowers enterprises to efficiently manage supply chain operations from warehouses to customers' doorsteps, to be best in its class.

“After an extensive selection process in looking to automate our warehouse, we chose Apptricity Jetstream as the best-of-breed solution,” Roberts said. “We are looking forward to our installation.”

Apptricity's Jetstream Warehouse Management handles receiving and put-away, inventory rotation and movement, fulfillment and shipping. Real-time data can be entered and accessed via smartphone as well as traditional hand-held scanners. Jetstream's recently released Smartfleet extends this robust offering to vehicle-based businesses.

“The real-time management of warehouse operations is critical in the optimization of supply-chain processes, and streamlining these functions has broad-reaching effects on the entire organization, from the safety of warehouse employees to customer satisfaction,” said Tim Garcia, Apptricity's CEO. “We are very excited about the opportunity to work with GDT and look forward to a bright future together.”
