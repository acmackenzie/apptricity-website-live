Apptricity, Janam Team Up to Deliver Superior Supply Chain Solution

DALLAS, Jan. 07, 2013 - Apptricity, provider of the Jetstream suite of supply chain, e-procurement, financial productivity and government services solutions, and Janam Technologies LLC, a leading provider of rugged mobile computers that scan barcodes and communicate wirelessly, announced today that Janam hardware has been certified for the Jetstream solution platform.

Apptricity's software delivers 360-degree visibility and mobile capabilities to supply chains, enabling enterprise customers to securely manage data, transactions and processes faster and more economically than competitive solutions. The certification of Janam's mobile-scanning capability increases the ranks of Apptricity's mobility partners, extending the reach of Jetstream to even more users. From mobile expense management to mobile travel and expense solutions, Apptricity Jetstream offers easy on-the-go integration.

Using Janam's purpose-built mobile computers, workers in retail, healthcare, hospitality, manufacturing and logistics can scan barcodes and instantly access mission-critical Apptricity applications for real-time intelligence in the field. Organizational leaders gain real-time data from the field that allows assets to be tracked throughout the supply chain for maximum efficiency.

“Jetstream's refined cloud-based architecture delivers ‘Above the Cloud' advantages by providing a global view of an organization's entire asset base, infrastructure, actions, transactions, tracking and activities,” said Tim Garcia, Apptricity's CEO. “This top-down view of crucial resources is the real-time intelligence needed for mission-critical decisions. Janam's rugged mobile computers deliver the processing power, screen-to-screen speed, enterprise-grade security and reliability needed for businesses to realize the full potential of Apptricity's applications. It's a powerful combination.”

“Like Janam, Apptricity aims to enable businesses of all sizes to streamline processes and operate more profitably,” said Harry B. Lerner, Janam's CEO. “Customers choose Janam because we deliver products with the right features and the right price. Apptricity's Jetstream provides end-to-end visibility and extends the value of IT investments. It's a win-win for customers, and we are pleased Apptricity selected Janam.”
