Southwire Expands Its Apptricity Solution Set with P-Card Management

DALLAS, June 4, 2013 – Apptricity, provider of the Jetstream suite of supply chain, e-procurement and financial productivity solutions, announced today that longtime partner Southwire Company has chosen to expand its Apptricity solutions set to include procurement card management.

Southwire, North America's largest wire and cable company, has employed Apptricity's Travel and Expense solution for 500 users since 2006. Now, Southwire will enjoy the benefit of procurement card activity that is consolidated in a single, comprehensive solution, with complete transparency and the ability to configure the solution to the company's business rules and requirements.

The expense and p-card solutions will be fully integrated, and with Apptricity P-Card Management, Southwire can be assured that transactions flow from company p-cards to the correct cost centers.

“We have come to rely on the transparency and control provided by Apptricity’s travel and expense solution, and now we look forward to realizing the same level of increased efficiency in P-Card Management,” said Ken Sayers, vice president of shared financial services at Southwire.

Apptricity p-card management automatically imports transactions into its expense management software on intervals defined by client needs. It supports p-cards from multiple vendors and tracks each transaction by ledger code. It also provides the ability to set procurement permission based on role, approved amount and geographic location.

Whether companies use purchase cards, travel cards, custodian cards, ghost cards or fleet cards – or a combination of the above – Apptricity's solution provides support, regardless of vendor.

“We're excited that Southwire liked our expense solution enough that it came back to Apptricity for p-card management,” said Tim Garcia, Apptricity’s CEO. “I look forward to many more years of helping Southwire enhance its financial productivity with ‘Above the Cloud’ transparency.”

About Southwire Company

A leader in technology and innovation, Southwire Company is North America's largest wire and cable company. Southwire and its subsidiaries manufacture building wire and cable, metal-clad (MC) cable, FlatWire Ready® products, cord products (including Tappan™ sound, security, and communication cables through Tappan Wire & Cable Inc.), utility cable products, industrial power cable, OEM wire products, SCR® copper and aluminum rod, and continuous casting technology. Southwire also supplies MAXIS® wire and cable installation tools to the commercial, industrial and utility construction markets.
