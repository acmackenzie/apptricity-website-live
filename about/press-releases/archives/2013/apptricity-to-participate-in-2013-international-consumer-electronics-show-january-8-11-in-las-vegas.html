Apptricity to Participate in 2013 International Consumer Electronics Show January 8-11 in Las Vegas

DALLAS, Jan. 4, 2013 – Apptricity, provider of the Jetstream suite of supply chain, e-procurement and financial productivity solutions, announced today it will participate in the 2013 International Consumer Electronics Show Jan. 8-11 in Las Vegas. The show is the year's most-anticipated technology-related trade show.

Apptricity's new Smartfleet application will be part of the ng Connect Program demonstrations in the Alcatel-Lucent booth #31412. Smartfleet uses Apptricity's Jetstream solution to extend the competitive advantage of wireless work order, inventory and warehouse management to commercial service providers, first responders and other vehicle-based businesses.

“We're excited for the chance to spread the word of our collaboration with the Alcatel-Lucent (Euronext and NYSE: ALU) ng Connect Program and to talk about Jetstream's ability to deliver ‘Above the Cloud' visibility to enterprise organizations,” said Tim Garcia, Apptricity's CEO. “The biggest tech show of the year is a great opportunity to reinforce how Smartfleet empowers vehicle-based businesses to operate more profitably and grow.”

Jetstream, Apptricity's revolutionary platform, unifies objectives for Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, and the military with accelerated business processes and command visibility.

The ng Connect Program, founded by Alcatel-Lucent, is a multi-industry ecosystem dedicated to the creation of the new generation connected user experience. ng Connect is comprised of more than 190 Contributing and Associate member companies, including leading network, consumer electronics, application and content providers. Twelve proof of concept demos will be featured this year in the Alcatel-Lucent CES booth.
