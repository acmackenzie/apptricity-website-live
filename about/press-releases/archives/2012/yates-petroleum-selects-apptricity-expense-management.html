Yates Petroleum Selects Apptricity Expense Management

Dallas, TX – March 8, 2012 – Apptricity Corporation, an industry leader in automated enterprise grade Supply Chain and Dynamic Spend Management solutions, announced today that Yates Petroleum has selected Apptricity Expense Management as its automated solution for travel and expense.

Yates Petroleum, one of the largest oil and gas producers in New Mexico, is known for its 75-year contribution to the industry as well as its leadership in promoting environmental and avian protection policies. Yates' selection of Apptricity Expense Management shows continued corporate adoption of Apptricity's SOA applications.

“It has been Apptricity's goal to provide its customers with “exact fit” solutions, rather than companies having to adapt to strict technology processes from SaaS and ERP companies,” said Tim Garcia, President and Chief Executive Officer of Apptricity Corporation.

With the addition of Yates Petroleum to its increasing list of clients, Apptricity continues expansion of its rapidly-deployable automated solutions for the energy industry, which includes clients such as TXU, Texas' largest energy provider.

About Apptricity
