Apptricity Jetstream Joins Motorola Solutions' PartnerEmpower™ Program

Dallas, TX – July 10, 2012 – Apptricity, provider of the innovative software application platform Jetstream, today announced its participation in the Motorola Solutions' PartnerEmpower™ program, focusing on converged communications, mobile computing, RFID and advanced logistics. PartnerEmpower offers Motorola channel partners an opportunity to specialize and demonstrate their expertise in technologies, applications and markets that will provide innovative solutions to end-user customers.

KEY FACTS

  • Apptricity is teaming with Motorola Solutions to help customers make critical decisions, streamline processes and improve results. With backing from the Motorola channel brand, Apptricity expects to grow geometrically, increase revenue and provide new software and device solutions for customers.
  • Apptricity chose Motorola Solutions for its ability to enhance market specialization and offer resources to further enhance Apptricity's 4G LTE expertise.
  • By participating in Motorola Solutions PartnerEmpower, Apptricity gains access to an innovative portfolio of products, along with comprehensive training, extensive marketing, sales and technical benefits.
  • The PartnerEmpower program provides access to certifications and an extensive set of tools that aim to differentiate Apptricity and help improve customer satisfaction.
  • PartnerEmpower is a global network that offers Apptricity opportunities for collaboration in delivering solutions for its current and potential customers.
Timothy Garcia, CEO, Apptricity, “Our relationship with Motorola Solutions is the start of a thrilling new ecosystem for Apptricity and our new Jetstream solution. We will work with Motorola to develop cutting-edge applications for its devices, including the ET1 Enterprise Tablet, to provide powerful mobile computing solutions for the federal government. By participating in the PartnerEmpower program, Apptricity can team with other world-class companies for global improvement opportunities. This collaboration will also streamline the application development process, which speeds up a product's entry into the marketplace.”
