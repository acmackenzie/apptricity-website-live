Apptricity Exceeds Year-End Revenue Goals for 2011

January 24, 2012 – Dallas, Texas – Apptricity Corporation, an industry leader in automated Supply Chain Management and Dynamic Spend Management solutions, announced today that its fourth quarter sales exceeded expectations to make 2011 a record year for revenues, profitability and employment.

“The strength of our automation technology for the enterprise continues to generate new business and expanding business with our existing customer base,” said Timothy D. Garcia, Chief Executive Officer & President of Apptricity. “Global name brand companies, government and growing organizations understand our value proposition goes beyond the initial implementation and it is one of the reasons our customers choose to expand with Apptricity.”

Apptricity new and existing customers contributing to Q4 revenue growth include AT&T, Reddy Ice, Helzberg Diamonds, Century Fasteners, ValueOptions, the Department of Defense. Apptricity Solutions are offered as on-premise or a SaaS Model. Our applications include Apptricity eProcurement, Apptricity Expense Management, Apptricity Invoice Management, Apptricity Asset Management, Apptricity Warehouse Management and other supply chain, finance and workforce management point solutions that automate manual processes through dynamic workflow, approval hierarchy, OCR extraction, business intelligence reporting and auditing.

About Apptricity

Founded in 1999, Apptricity Corporation provides Supply Chain Management and Dynamic Spend Management solutions. Customers benefit from reduced operational costs due to improvements in efficiencies and compliance. Apptricity also offers multiple deployment alternatives to address customer demands including a perpetual license and Software-as-a-Service (SaaS). Built on an advanced Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA) framework, Apptricity solutions provide platform independence, broad-reaching integration, mass customization and hassle-free migration.
