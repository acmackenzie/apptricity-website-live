Apptricity Continues Record Growth in Q1 2012

Dallas, TX - June 1, 2012 - “We continue to see strong demand from organizations seeking to increase visibility through more granular asset management and real-time logistics, as well as organizations looking to improve productivity through automated procurement, expenses control and compliance,” said Timothy D. Garcia, Chief Executive Officer.

“Renewal rate in our SaaS subscription business was 100% during the quarter.” Q1-2012 saw a forty percent increase in year over year operating revenues as multiple customers extended and expanded their relationship with Apptricity.
