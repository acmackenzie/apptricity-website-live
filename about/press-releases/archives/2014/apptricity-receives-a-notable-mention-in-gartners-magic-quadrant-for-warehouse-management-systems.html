Apptricity Receives a Notable Mention in Gartner's Magic Quadrant for Warehouse Management Systems

Apptricity’s agility and flexibility highlighted in Gartner’s latest Magic Quadrant for Warehouse Management Systems report.

Dallas, TX (PRWEB) October 16, 2014

Apptricity, leading global provider of innovative mobile enterprise software, including mission-critical supply chain management and integrated financial management solutions, recently received a notable mention in Gartner’s Magic Quadrant for Warehouse Management Systems report, which was published on Tuesday, September 30, 2014.

“It is a an honor for us, a SMB vendor, to be included in a marquee note for Gartner’s Magic Quadrant for Warehouse Management Systems,” said Diane Ianni, vice president of marketing at Apptricity. “We’re pleased that Gartner’s research analyst also differentiated us as ‘well positioned in asset management, defense and related government logistics environments.’ Our strengths in these areas make us a great fit for a wide variety of enterprise clients.”

Apptricity Warehouse Management covers core warehouse management system capabilities with some extended capabilities. The user-friendly software helps manage incoming shipments, putaway operations, inventory rotation and movement, fulfillment and shipping. Specialized capabilities help minimize lost revenue and leverage GPS-based RFID technology.

Features within Apptricity Warehouse Management include:

  • Inbound item tracking with configurable validation points and integration with enterprise systems
  • Full physical inventory capabilities and configurable options for managing cycle counts
  • Complete kitting capabilities and configurable options for bills of materials and product routings
  • Support for compliance labeling and RFID tagging
  • System-directed wireless putaway with configurable rules and validation points.

“Our warehouse management solution uses cutting-edge technology to meet the challenge of efficiently managing inventory in any warehouse,” added Tim Garcia, founder and CEO of Apptricity. “We’re pleased that Gartner acknowledges our unique stance in the marketplace with the various software solutions we can offer in addition to warehouse management.”
