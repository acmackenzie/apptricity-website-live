Executive Team

Timothy D. Garcia, Chief Executive Officer & President
Tim Garcia brings more than 25 years of software sales, management and development experience in the enterprise applications market to Apptricity. Under his leadership, Apptricity’s growing client base now includes three of the world’s largest organizations, namely Walmart Stores, AT&T and the Department of Defense. Prior to Apptricity, Garcia held management positions at Pivotal Corporation (Nasdaq: PVTL), Compuware Corporation (Nasdaq: CPWR), Peregrine Systems (NYSE: HPQ), Sterling Software (NYSE: CA), and EDS (NYSE: HPQ). He received his BA degree in Economics from the University of California at Davis.
Randall S. Lieberman, Chief Financial Officer
Randy Lieberman has approximately 25 years experience in finance, sales and marketing. Prior to Apptricity, Lieberman led Apptricity's initial institutional capital raise as a general partner with Quarry Venture Partners. Before Quarry, Lieberman was a partner with Vision Capital Partners and a vice president with Chase Securities Inc. (NYSE: JPM), where he structured and executed numerous merger, acquisition and project finance transactions in energy and real estate. His background also includes sales and marketing in the consumer packaged goods industry while at Nestle (ADR: NSRGY). He received his MBA from University of Texas at Austin and his BSM degree from Tulane University.
Ralph McCoy, Chief Technology Officer
Ralph McCoy leads Apptricity's Government services division after heading up Apptricity's development group and its enterprise applications group. He brings more than 20 years of technology development and consulting experience in the enterprise applications market. Prior to co-founding Apptricity, McCoy helped form NetLinx, a subsidiary of Hillenbrand Industries (NYSE: HB), which provided advanced communication and location tracking solutions to the healthcare industry. He managed the development of multiple web and wireless based products, including a Healthcare Information Exchange Network. Before NetLinx, he was a senior manager with First Consulting Group, performing technology assessments related to acquisitions and new product ventures for established and emerging technology companies. McCoy received his BS degree in Computer Science from University of Central Oklahoma.
Roger Sanford, Senior Vice President, Strategic Alliances
Roger Sanford has been a leader in the marketing industry for over 25 years. He was CMO at PGP, Mbrane, Centura, 3Market and Infreeda, and also performed acquisition branding work for PeopleSoft, Varian and Vantive. Roger has provided compelling & innovative solutions and sustainable result-based programs for companies including Alcatel Lucent, Apple, AT&T, BT, Chevron, eBay, Epson, HP, IBM, Intel, Kodak, Mercedes-Benz, Match.com, Nokia, Samsung, Skyy Vodka (created the blue bottle), Siemens, Sony, UPS, Varian, Verizon and Volvo. His undergraduate work was at University of California at Santa Cruz. His MBA graduate work was with the Levy School Of Business at Santa Clara University, where he teaches classes in marketing.
Mark J. Oliver, Executive Vice President Commercial Services
Mark Oliver has more than 20 years of software sales experience in the enterprise applications market, with a recognized track record for success in building and developing technical organizations. Prior to Apptricity, Oliver was the Director of North American Application Specialists at Pivotal Corporation (Nasdaq: PVTL), an enterprise eCRM provider. Oliver quickly built and managed a team to support the entire sales operation across the United States and Canada, supporting the company’s fastest growing sales team. He has also held key positions at enterprise software companies, including Cognos Corporation (Nasdaq: COGN), Compuware Corporation (Nasdaq: CPWR), and Uniface. Oliver received his Information Systems degree from James Madison University in Virginia.
Diane Mitchell, Vice President, Marketing
Diane Mitchell joined the Apptricity team as Vice President of Marketing in August 2014. Diane has over 20 years of experience in the software and services industry including marketing, product management, strategic channel alliances, sales and technical support. She has marketing and leadership experience with start-ups to enterprise companies including i2 Technologies, CA, Goal Systems, Legent Corporation, Platinum technology, Systems Center and Rockwell International. She received her BS in Mathematics and Computer Science from Bethany College.
Todd Wiegand, Vice President, Application Development
Todd Wiegand brings more than 35 years of information technology (IT) industry experience to his role as VP of application development. He spent the last 25-plus years in the supply chain management and execution space working with clients across a variety of industries. Todd served as supply chain architect at Apptricity prior to heading up product management for the application suite. Since then, he has combined his capabilities to oversee all application development for Apptricity’s supply chain management and spend management solution suites, emphasizing a holistic vision for development. Prior to his tenure at Apptricity, Todd has held significant roles in implementations, alliances, sales and marketing, field education, and leadership. His international experience spans more than 20 countries. Todd holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Information Systems.
Terry Cave, Senior Vice President, EMEA
Terry brings more than 25 years senior management experience to Apptricity, and provides significant value in his role to grow and develop the EMEA region. Prior to Apptricity, Terry has served his time in major international organisations such as Siebel, Datacash, vc-net and Centura Software, at Board level and in both Executive and non-Executive roles. Terry's particular skills and experience from within the technology and communications industry focus on building businesses, and his proven international credentials, having worked across Europe, the US, Asia Pacific, the Middle East and Africa, are essential in his role as SVP EMEA.
Lewis Henderson, Vice President, Channels
Lewis has over 15 years executing at management level, and brings to Apptricity a wealth of experience to deliver against the corporate strategy of Channel business growth. Lewis' background includes reputed global organizations such as Kaspersky and Celestix, where he designed and structured successful Channel programs, delivering value to partners and the business. Prior to being commercially focused, Lewis spent time as a Technical lead, where he earned several Information Security accreditations across leading vendors such as Check Point, RSA, Websense and Clearswift.
