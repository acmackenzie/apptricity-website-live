Controller

At Apptricity, we are pioneering radical shifts in the enterprise software experience. Apptricity application suites focus on finance, supply chain, and workforce management. Our Finance department is looking for a Controller.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Report to CEO and CFO
  • Generate annual, quarterly, monthly financial statements
  • Track and report franchise sales taxes and other regulatory filings
  • Track, measure and report capitalization and amortization of research and development costs
  • Track, measure and report capitalization and deprecation of hardware [server, computer, screens, etc] investments
  • Review revenue billing [maintenance and SaaS] reports generated by Assistant Controller
  • Review accounts payable and accounts receivable tracking reports generated by Assistant Controller
  • Review vacation, sick, and other leave tracking reports generated by Human Resources Director
  • Develop division budget reports, specifically IT and marketing
  • Developer division-based bulling reports which identify WIP completed, billed and unbilled capacity, and compensated overages
  • Develop workforce efficiency reports comparing salary and contractor effort versus associated billing using time reports quarterly, monthly, weekly
  • Develop sales commission reports quarterly and weekly
  • Assist in modeling new product and bundled pricing
  • Track, measure and report sales information to identify trends and opportunities

Minimum Requirements:

  • Outstanding communication and writing abilities
  • Revenue recognition experience in the technology sector
  • Experience generating and filing federal tax returns and other compliance reports
  • Minimum 8 years work experience in accounting
  • Certified Public Accountant
