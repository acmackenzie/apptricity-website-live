Sales Representative

Apptricity is currently looking to add experienced Sales Representatives to add to our Direct and Indirect sales team, based from our corporate headquarters in Dallas (Irving) Texas. We are looking for a seasoned representative with a minimum of five years sales experience in one of the following areas: ERP, Financial, or Supply Chain Management applications. Will be responsible for establishing, maintaining or expanding relationships and actively developing Apptricity sales with named accounts or all accounts and strategic partners within the given territory.

Responsibilities:

  • Apply consultative and strategic selling skills.
  • Show technical expertise to selling, developing and managing a customer base.
  • Address existing customers and non customers through direct, face to face contacts.
  • Develop long term and valuable partnerships with local distributors for specific territory sections.
  • Follow a sales strategy, initiate and develop contact and consulting on a regular basis with existing clients.
  • Understand customers’ and partners’ strategic business objectives.
  • Evaluate customers and prospects information strategies and solutions in relation to those objectives.
  • Develop proposals to meet those objectives.
  • Sell recommendations at the appropriate level to the customer’s organization.
  • Provide accurate, timely forecasts to all levels of upper management.
  • Develop and cultivate territory development, direct and indirect sales.
  • Select relevant customer’s accounts to achieve assigned sales quota.
  • Analyze customer requirements and promote consideration of Apptricity solutions.
  • Actively participate in the preparation and execution of sales plans, events and campaigns.

Professional Skills:

  • Knowledge and familiarity with most computing environments.
  • Effective and efficient selling, presentation and marketing skills, including thorough knowledge of marketing practices and operations for large accounts approaching and selling.
  • Working knowledge and understanding of the Enterprise market and evolution.

Required Skills:

  • Proven track record of achieving annual sales targets.
  • Effective time management.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, interpersonal skills, and decision making skills.
  • Professional appearance and presentation required.
  • Able to work in fast paced sales environment.
  • Travel up to 50%

Note: Local candidates only, no relocation assistance provided.
