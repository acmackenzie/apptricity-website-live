Quality Assurance Manager

At Apptricity, we are pioneering radical shifts in the enterprise software experience. Apptricity application suites focus on finance, supply chain, and workforce management. Our solutions optimize customer resources, reduce operational costs, and can be customized to any type of business. Apptricity technology is so flexible and adaptable that it can be configured, integrated, and deployed faster than any enterprise solution currently on the market.

Apptricity is searching for an experienced QA Manager to lead a team of QA professionals. The QA Manager will have direct oversight all projects, performance, development, and training for the QA department. This person will also serve as the primary point of contact for senior management reporting and department-wide feedback. The QA team has responsibly for both manual and automation testing across all of software products, both in the base applications and customer specific scenarios. The QA Manager will liaise with other managers and staff throughout the organization to ensure that QA processes are functioning properly and problems are resolved.

Responsibilities:

  • Supervise all Quality Assurance Analysts, ensuring tasks and procedures are properly understood, carried out, and evaluated
  • Set QA compliance objectives and ensure targets are achieved
  • Set standards and establishing clearly defined quality methods for team members to apply
  • Mentor team leads and team members, monitoring their performance and providing constructive feedback and direction
  • Take direction from senior management and ensure implementation of directives within established timelines, providing periodic updates on status, identifying any issues and suggesting resolution
  • Maintain current workflow controls and procedures and provide feedback on necessary changes
  • Write clear and precise test cases, test plans and test scripts based on testing needs and results
  • Test software functionality by executing functional, application, regression, and performance tests for software releases
  • Analyzes test results and makes the necessary corrections to procedures or duties if needed
  • Become a user expert on how our products work, gaining an understanding of the strengths and weaknesses and championing customer issues to ensure they have a positive experience
  • Assist product support with analysis and verification of software defects reported
  • Promote quality achievement and performance improvement throughout the organization
  • Other duties as assigned

Experienced Required:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science/Information Systems, and/or 8-10 years related experience
  • Minimum 2 years of supervisory or leadership experience
  • Minimum 2 years of experience using Quality Center and QTP automation testing tool
  • Strong understanding of QA processes and the Software Development Lifecycle

Characteristics of Successful Candidate:

  • Excellent analytical skills and strong attention to detail
  • Able to reinforce thoughts and ideas of others and challenge assumptions
  • Ability to follow direction/issues through to resolution
  • Manage and prioritize changing tasks and displays commitment by adopting an ‘extra mile’ attitude
  • Exhibits high level of enthusiasm and expressiveness
  • Displays passion for quality and champions quality of the user experience
  • Build and maintain rapport with different teams and adapt communication style accordingly
  • Eager to take personal responsibility for the quality of our products and to ensure all product releases are thoroughly tested, robust, and conform to specification

Note: Permanent Residency Required. U.S. citizenship may be required for security clearance.
