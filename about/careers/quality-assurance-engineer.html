Quality Assurance Engineer

Apptricity is looking for a Quality Assurance Superstar to work as a member of our growing team. Working with other QA engineers, you will perform a variety of QA tasks, perform manual testing, and build automated test scripts when needed. Every feature and function of the software must be completely tested so that any potential issue is identified and fixed before the product goes live. The software should be tested in terms of functionality, performance, reliability, stability and compatibility with other legacy and / or external systems. This position requires a self-motivated, dynamic individual with strong technical and communication skills. We are looking for someone who takes initiative to find new and improved ways to get things done, is action oriented and achieves results.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Build manual test scripts for customer specific processes and functionality
  • Create user based scenarios with variable data insertion
  • Define standard regression and stress test approaches
  • Report and track bugs and third-party issues with a high degree of detail
  • Work independently and take initiatives to solve problems and QA challenges
  • Communicate issues, risks and test status in a timely, detailed manner across working teams and functional boundaries
  • Function as a contributing member of a SCRUM team
  • Proactively verify contributions from the development team to the QA test environment
  • Be responsible for the health and effectiveness of the QA test environment

Required Skills:

  • 1-3 years of overall experience as part of a software QA team
  • BS in Computer Science or equivalent work experience
  • Strong understanding of SQA methodologies and practices
  • Experience with RDBMS data structures
  • Experience with SQL queries to verify test results
  • Demonstrated ability to own a complete functional area of an application or product
  • Demonstrated ability to work with limited software specifications and define automated testing strategies and test cases for assigned product components
  • Excellent interpersonal, written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to creatively solve technical problems within deadlines
  • Familiar with automated testing a plus

Note: Permanent Residency Required. U.S. citizenship may be required for security clearance.
