Receptionist

Apptricity Corporation is looking for a front desk superstar, to join our growing team. The Receptionist is responsible for professionally and efficiently managing visitors, telephone calls and messages, as well as performing a variety of clerical duties. The Receptionist is the voice of the company.

Responsibilities:

  • Receive and direct all telephone calls and visitors, promptly, accurately, professionally and courteously. Must become an expert at using all features of the telephone system and voicemail.
  • Prepare and process outgoing and incoming mail and deliveries, including signing for deliveries and notifying recipients.
  • Ensure that common areas, including kitchen and conference room, are kept neat, clean, and stocked with appropriate supplies.
  • Order office supplies and keep supply cabinet organized and stocked.
  • Plan and assist in organizing departmental and company luncheons by contacting caterers, getting quotes, placing orders, etc.
  • Schedule internal meetings and keep track of the conference/training room calendars.
  • Provide ongoing communication with immediate supervisor, keeping her well informed of activities and identifying potential problems. Include recommended corrective actions where possible.
  • Provide support to Accounting

Minimum Requirements:

  • Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel and Outlook are required
  • Experience as a receptionist in a professional office environment preferred.
  • Strong communication skills both oral and written.
  • Requires attention to detail.
  • Outgoing, engaging personality.
  • Eagerness to assist others and learn new skills.
