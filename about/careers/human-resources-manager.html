Human Resources Manager

Human Resources Manager will report to the Director of Corporate Services and perform day-to-day HR/benefit operations.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Recruiting and Employee Retention
    • Improve recruiting process, focusing on testing, interviewing and counseling hiring managers on interview conduct and candidate selection
    • Ensure new hire requisitions and offer letters are properly completed and approved
    • Plan, coordinate and conduct new employee orientation and on-boarding, including communication of company and benefits plan policies, features, enrollment, and other requirements
    • Manage existing and develop new programs/training to improve employee satisfaction, retention and ensure continued employee professional development
    • Manage relationships with Employment Search Firms, ensuring candidates match internal requirements
    • Conduct and analyze exit interviews, providing suggestions on improvements
    • Work with employee engagement committee, mentoring members and providing oversight
  • Benefits Administration
    • Manage employee benefit programs (medical, dental, vision, life, disability, retirement, and PTO plans)
    • Take the lead during open enrollment, providing information and answering questions
    • Research and respond to internal and external benefit inquiries throughout the year
    • Coordinate with carrier representatives to resolve complex benefit issues including, but not limited to, eligibility discrepancies and benefit plan interpretations
    • Work with carrier representatives yearly to ensure we get the best possible plans for our employees
    • Work with Accounting to reconcile enrollment reports and billings to employees to ensure accurate eligibility and payment information and compliance with contract provisions
    • Ensure employee information is kept up-to-date and accurate on all benefit policies
  • Employee Relations/Coaching
    • Ensure job descriptions and job requirements are kept up-to-date for all positions
    • Enable the proper planning, monitoring and appraisal of employees by training managers how to coach and discipline their staff
    • Manage and resolve employee grievances, ensuring everything is documented and properly resolved
    • Oversee the employee annual review process, ensuring reviews are conducted timely and that the employee’s goals are definable and trackable to promote employee growth
  • Legal/Reporting Requirements
    • Gather information, file reports and track progress to comply with all required local, state, and Federal programs (e.g. COBRA, HIPPA, ACA and ERISA) and all other necessary reporting
  • HR Management
    • Process and maintain all HR information (e.g. provider contracts, benefit claim files, employee records, new hire paperwork, etc.) for the purpose of providing an up-to-date reference and audit trail
    • Prepare, update and recommend human resource policies and procedures, including updates/distribution of employee handbook and all other relevant policies
    • Research and recommend a new HRIS system

Required Qualifications:

  • 5+ years HR management experience, including recruiting, benefits administration, performance management and legal requirements
  • 2+ years managing support staff
  • Minimum 4 year college degree required
  • Must have at least one HR certification (HR MBA, PHR, SPHR, etc.)
  • Must have experience working for a software or high tech company
  • Excellent organizational and software skills with attention to detail
  • Excellent negotiation and problem solving skills
  • Outstanding communication skills both oral and written
  • High level of integrity, discretion and proven experience successfully managing confidential information
  • Proven ability to manage multiple tasks in a fast paced environment
