Database Report Writer

Apptricity Corporation is actively seeking a Database Report Writer to join our growing team. This role calls for a thorough, detailed, creative and sharp analytical mind. The Report Writer will have the primary responsibility for report development/analysis, including data validation and will be working on the reengineering of legacy reports and/or the development of new reports using Jaspersoft Report Studio as a member of the Business Intelligence Team. The report developer/analyst will also be responsible for analyzing the report results to ensure accuracy, and data knowledge.

The report analyst/developer will engage internal, and possibly external, clients in report writing and analytical efforts via corporate sponsored projects or production support requests routed through our request management system

Key Responsibilities:

  • Work directly with requirement analyst, architect, technical lead and other team members on design and development tasks.
  • Provide support to ensure optimal performance of reports and dashboards.
  • Collaborate with others to identify report requirements, design features and specifications.
  • Convert functional specifications/user stories into report design.
  • Design and code medium-to-high complexity reports leveraging Jaspersoft.
  • Conduct job duties and responsibilities according to Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC) methodology.
  • Document system design, report specifications and unit testing information and transfer maintenance work to Client production support team.
  • Conduct report specification and code review, unit and integration testing.
  • Examines/evaluates purpose and content of business reports to develop new, or improve existing, format, use and control.
  • Reviews report specifications to determine basic characteristics, such as origin and report flow, format, data elements, frequency, distribution, and purpose of report function.
  • Gather user requirements; analyze user requirements based on findings in scope documents.
  • Prepare and issue instructions concerning generation, completion, and distribution of reports according to new or revised practices, procedures, or policies of reports management.
  • Understand and comprehends system changes as they affect data, reports, and reporting systems.
  • Perform ad-hoc analytical requests and research projects assigned by management.
  • Troubleshoot BI tools/system; performance-tune these applications as necessary.
  • Make recommendation to leadership on process improvements or tool enhancements.

Required Skills:

  • Bachelor's Degree, or equivalent experience in computer science, programming, informatics, or business, or 4 years professional experience in report writing.
  • 1-2 years’ experience in programming in a language such as SQL, or similar experience in an analytical toolset
  • 1-2 years hands-on experience in a recognized reporting tool set such as Jaspersoft, SSRS, or Crystal Reports (Studio/Server, Domains, Ad-Hoc Views and Dashboards).
  • Strong report layout and presentation skills
  • Understanding of report server integration with web application
  • Experience handling and producing different file formats: CSV, XML, HTML, XLSX and PDF among others.
  • Understanding of J2EE technologies
  • Solid SQL knowledge
  • Experience in database schema and data warehousing
  • Ability to analyze user requirements
  • Excellent written and verbal communication
  • Ability to take initiative and accountability for results.
  • Demonstrated ability to work in a calm and professional manner in stressful situations
  • Strong track record of success with owning solutions from concept to completion
  • Exceptional analytical, conceptual, and problem-solving abilities
  • Able to prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment
  • Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment with good interpersonal skills
  • Ability to interact professionally with a diverse group, including executives, managers and subject matter experts.
  • Experience with common Java application development related technologies – Servlets, XML, JSP, HTML, XHTML, JRXML, CSS, Java script, Spring & Hibernate

Permanent Residency Required. U.S. citizenship may be required for security clearance.
