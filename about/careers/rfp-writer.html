Content Manager/RFP Writer

Apptricity is seeking to add a multi-talented writer that cannot only produce quality technical documents, but also have experience with capturing, documenting, and editing requirements from technical and business teams in an RFP development effort. Our ideal candidate will be a writer that has a persuasive writing style which generates interest in our products or services, for the specific intent of increasing revenue. The writer will work closely with Sales Officers, Sales Associates, Product Managers and Professional Services to ensure that information is correct and to help produce high-quality proposals.

Job Duties:

Qvidian® Content Manager

  • Maintain the proposal content library using Qvidian®
  • Create and manage document architecture for proposalautomation in Qvidian®
  • Create proposal responses by answering questions using Qvidian®
  • Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; maintaining networks and sharing knowledge with team.

RFP Writing

  • Produce and deliver technically accurate, high quality, on-time documentation based on a thorough understanding of internal/external expectations
  • Meets proposal deadline by establishing priorities and target dates for information gathering, writing, review, and approval; entering and monitoring tracking data; coordinating requirements with contributors; contributing proposal status information to review meetings; transmitting proposals.
  • Develops proposal by assembling information including project nature, objectives/outcomes/deliverables, implementation, methods, timetable, staffing, budget, standards of performance, and evaluation; writing, revising, and editing drafts including executive summaries, conclusions, and organization credentials.
  • Work to become a subject matter expert (SME) by conducting thorough research and building quality relationships with internal and external SMEs
  • Improves proposal-writing results by evaluating and re-designing processes, approach, coordination, and boilerplate; implementing changes.
  • Periodically review and update text through collaboration with SMEs, other technical writers, and graphic designer
  • Perform other duties as assigned

Knowledge and Professional Skills:

  • Understanding of Qvidian® proposal automation is preferred
  • Advanced understanding of a variety of documentation tools including Microsoft Office, Acrobat Professional, and Snagit
  • Military knowledge/experience and experience in writing/preparing Government RFP's is preferred
  • Superior technical writing skills and knowledge of industry best practices
  • Must have experience in delivering content in a variety of formats
  • Strong interviewing and listening skills with the ability to map complex technical information
  • Proficient project management skills and the ability to manage several tasks simultaneously
  • Has knowledge of commonly used procurement concepts, practices and procedures relative to RFP

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's Degree with a minimum of two years RFP writing experience
  • Advanced understanding of MS Word styles and other formatting features is required
  • Must have high attention to detail
  • Must be comfortable working in a fast-paced, deadline driven environment
