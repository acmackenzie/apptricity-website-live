Windows Mobile Developer

Apptricity Corporation is looking for a skilled Windows Mobile developer to help expand its mobile solutions. The ideal candidate will have experience with Windows Mobile development and also be interested in becoming proficient in additional mobile platforms. Apptricity produces leading edge finance and supply chain management software utilizing state of the art technology.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in the design and development of Apptricity mobile applications
  • Develop detailed knowledge of all Apptricity products, product architecture and development tools
  • Program efficiently using OOP and unit testing principles
  • Participate in an Agile project management team by being responsive and collaborative
  • Fix bugs, troubleshoot/debug production issues and participate in the release process

Required Skills:

  • Windows Mobile 6 experience
  • 2+ years C# programming experience
  • University degree in Computer Science or related field

Desired Skills:

  • Windows Phone 8 experience
  • Experience with optimizing mobile applications
  • Experience integrating SOAP and RESTful web services
  • Experience with mobile relational databases and off-line data syncing patterns
  • Knowledge of mobile device encryption techniques
  • Experience with SVN or Git
  • Experience with an Agile team development environment
  • Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written
  • Excellent problem solving, analytical, and troubleshooting skills

City: Irving
State/Province: Texas
Country: United States
Job type: Full Time
