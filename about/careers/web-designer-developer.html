Web Designer / Developer

At Apptricity, we are pioneering radical shifts in the mobile enterprise software experience. Apptricity application suites focus on finance, supply chain, and workforce management solutions. Our innovative products optimize customer resources, reduce operational costs, and are customizable to fit any type of business model.

We are searching for a Web Designer with strong development skills to join our Marketing team. You will help plan, implement and maintain Apptricity’s website so that it is easy to search, fully responsive, and visually effective in attracting and keeping visitors.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's Degree with a focus in Computer Science or related degree
  • 3-5 years of experience in the software or technology industry
  • Proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery, API's, and Adobe Creative Suite
  • At least 3 years managing and developing large-scale websites
  • Candidate must have a strong understanding of cross-browser compatibility, responsive design, general web functions and standards
  • Ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously and manage time effectively
  • Ability to self-manage time and work independently; deadline and results oriented
  • Must be detail-oriented, poised and composed under pressure
  • Ability to lead and follow projects from concept to completion
  • Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to take direction and collaborate

Responsibilities:

  • Applying best practices in user interface and interactive design, including image optimization and site mapping
  • Gathering, planning and delivering web solutions used across multiple devices
  • Creating and modifying the appearance of the website
  • Constantly improving SEO and analyzing site traffic
  • Determining all necessary coding requirements for changes, updates, and redesign
  • Creating and optimizing landing pages for all campaigns
  • Aligning the website with Apptricity’s business goals and marketing strategies, and ensure the design is of the highest quality
  • Learning about our company’s products, mission, and solutions by conducting thorough research and building quality relationships with internal and external SMEs
  • Planning new site designs by gathering input from SMEs, clarifying goals along the way; designing for functionality
  • Developing site navigation by categorizing content
  • Establishing “locatability” by registering with search engines
  • Monitoring performance and results; identifying and evaluating improvement options; introducing new technology; maintaining links
  • Updating your job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations

Desired Skills:

  • Deep functional knowledge or hands on design experience with Web Services (like Hubspot)
  • Strong grasp of security principles and how they apply to web solutions

Candidates without portfolios will not be considered.
