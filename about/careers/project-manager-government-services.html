Project Manager Government Services

Apptricity Corp, a leading supplier of mission critical supply chain solutions, is expanding our project management team. We are looking for a highly motivated, self starter to lead customer implementations of our Government Services. The Project Manager will oversee the technical application design, construction and implementation for assigned Government and Commercial contracts.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with developers, quality assurance, production support, and account teams to provide technical direction on systems analysis, design, development, testing and deployment.
  • Ensure timely and effective delivery of the solution into production.
  • Document technical requirements, develop and oversee project plans and implement change control procedures.
  • Facilitate code reviews, software design sessions, and architectural reviews.
  • Participate in technical advisory roles, as required.
  • Interface with customers and stakeholders to set objectives and evaluate performance.
  • Ensure effective handoff to operations for ongoing maintenance and troubleshooting.
  • Ensure an increase level of code reuse, and contribute to the growth and use of the knowledge library.
  • Mentor associates and foster a learning and growth environment.

Requirements

Minimum Requirements:

  • A Bachelors degree in a technical discipline or equivalent.
  • Demonstrated skills with project management tools and methodologies, software development lifecycle, and problem solving.
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects. Ability to meet stated customer initiated deadlines.
  • Previous work experience with Government travel policies and ability to provide Subject Matter Expertise (SME) on business rules and requirements needed in governmental travel processing automation.
  • Security clearance eligibility required
  • Two to five years experience as a technical project manager or project leader in an online commerce or software venue, scrum master experience/certification preferred.

Desired Requirements

  • Security Clearance
  • PMP Certification
  • Experience implementing product using XML, Java, UNIX, Oracle and DB2.
