Implementation Technician

Apptricity Corporation is seeking an experienced Implementation Support Technician to provide superior customer support to our customers. The ideal candidate will provide technical customer support related activities, including but not limited to software implementation support, issue tracing and resolution, investigating production issues, and installing/deploying/configuring our application software. These intercalations will take place both over the phone and via email.

The Implementation Support Technician will also be responsible for carrying out software testing activities related projects ensure all deliverables are released as specified with the highest quality.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Install and Configure software applications to run under a variety of Application servers on different OS platforms
  • Under minimal supervision, support Customers in resolving Production and Software implementation issues
  • Communicate in all forms with the Customer in order to help resolve IT and software issues and/or understand software operating requirements.
  • Write clear and precise defect reports as required for identified software defects and functional defects based on testing results
  • Become a user expert on how our products work, gaining an understanding of the strengths and weaknesses; while championing customer issues in order to ensure they have a positive experience
  • Other duties as assigned

Minimum Requirements:

  • Experience with Apache Tomcat
  • Strong SQL knowledge
  • No less than three years related experience providing Technical Support
  • Experience in deploying and configuring web-based applications
  • Exposure to SSL and other data security methods
  • Understanding of both Windows and Linux OS
  • Exposure of issue management and bug lifecycle is required
  • Strong understanding of QA processes and the Software Development Lifecycle
  • Excellent analytical skills
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Able to think creatively and propose options/solutions to challenges
  • Ability to quickly learn new software products

Desired Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science/Information Systems and three years related experience
  • Recent experience performing Quality Assurance activates
  • JIRA Administration strongly desired
  • Able to reinforce thoughts and ideas of others and challenge assumptions
  • Ability to follow issues through to resolution
  • Can manage and prioritize tasks with a sense of urgency

Permanent Residency Required. U.S. citizenship may be required for security clearance.
