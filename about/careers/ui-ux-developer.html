UX/UI Developer

Apptricity Corporation is seeking a UX/UI Developer to join our User Experience team. We are a rapidly growing software development company in Las Colinas specializing in business process automation software. We are looking for passionate candidates that think outside the box and are not afraid of exploring new territory in the pursuit of better user experience.

As a UX-UI designer, you do not just make things look nice but your primary focus is to understand stakeholders and users, to extract business goals and ensure our apps are useable, intuitive and intelligent. Explore and capture what users are looking for and align with business goals. Create compelling designs across web-based and mobile platforms (responsive), focus on developing a consistent “Apptricity“ brand and experience, create story boards, wireframes and mockups, design interactions and flows through the application and assist in creating UI/UX prototypes. Seek to understand the user and drive towards an experience that is simple yet complete with respect to solving business problems. Must be a self-starter, friendly, able to communicate and present to executives, managers, developers and end-users.

All candidates must present a comprehensive portfolio in web, print, or PDF format to be considered and may be asked to complete a skills test. Candidates without portfolios will not be considered.

Requirements:

  • Bachelors Degree or equivalent experience in related roles
  • Highly proficient with Adobe Creative Suite CS5/CS6 and at least one other prototyping tool
  • 2-5 years of experience in software product design, development, and software product launch

Responsibilities

  • Solid understanding of the principles behind user experience, web navigation, user habits, current design trends, cross-browser and platform compatibility issues
  • Create multiple wireframe designs demonstrating alternative solutions to product requirements
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment, while remaining flexible, receptive, and adaptive to change
  • Proactively seek product requirements, understand/create personas, and evaluate users feedback for implementation
  • Must be able to prioritize daily, weekly, and monthly tasks and projects effectively

Desired Skills

  • Experience working with Product Manager in a software life cycle
  • Experience in Agile software development preferred
  • Experience with mobile design and development preferred
  • Experience with HTML5, responsive design, and JS preferred
  • Knowledge of visual design principles including typography, color, and icon development a plus
  • Ability to utilize and hand code valid, accessible HTML5 and CSS3

Permanent Residency Required. U.S. citizenship may be required for security clearance.
