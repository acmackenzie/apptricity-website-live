iOS Mobile Developer

Apptricity Corporation is looking for a skilled mobile developer to help expand its mobile solutions. The ideal candidate will have experience with mobile development and also be interested in learning new hardware platforms. Apptricity produces leading edge software utilizing state of the art technology.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in the design and development of Apptricity mobile applications
  • Become knowledgeable in the Software/Hardware architecture of Apptricity products, including system configuration, database schema, functions, indexes, and views
  • Program efficiently using OOP principles
  • Work within project teams, and customer service teams as a technical resource and contribute to successful implementations and customer satisfaction
  • Work in a team environment to maximize customer satisfaction to develop strong customer references
  • Develop detailed knowledge of all Apptricity products, product architecture and development tools
  • Provide assistance with testing and problem resolution as may be required

Required Skills:

  • University degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 1-3 years experience working Objective-C
  • Experience with developing native applications for iOS, Android or Windows Mobile

Desired Skills:

  • Experience with optimizing web applications for access on mobile devices
  • Experience with REST/SOAP Web services
  • Familiarity with encryption and security procedures
  • Experience using SQL, SQL Server, SQLite, Oracle, or DB2
  • Experience with source control software (preferably SVN)
  • Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written
  • Excellent problem solving abilities
