Project Manager Applications

Apptricity Corp, a leading supplier of supply chain, finance, and workforce applications, is expanding our project management team. We are looking for a highly motivated, self starter to lead customer implementations of our enterprise resource solutions. The Project Manager will oversee the technical application design, construction and implementation for assigned accounts and projects.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with developers, quality assurance, production support, and account teams to provide technical direction on systems analysis, design, development, testing and deployment.
  • Ensure timely and effective delivery of the solution into production.
  • Document technical requirements, develop and oversee project plans and implement change control procedures.
  • Facilitate code reviews, software design sessions, and architectural reviews.
  • Participate in technical advisory roles, as required.
  • Interact with customers at the technical level, as required.
  • Ensure effective handoff to operations for ongoing maintenance and troubleshooting.
  • Ensure an increase level of code reuse, and contribute to the growth and use of the knowledge library.
  • Mentor associates and foster a learning and growth environment.

Requirements

Minimum Requirements:

  • A Bachelors degree in a technical discipline or equivalent.
  • Two to five years experience using web development tools and architectures such as: J2EE, Visual Basic, C++, SQL Server, ASP, OOD, and COM.
  • Two to five years experience on multiple platforms: Web Servers, NT, Windows 2000, and Application Servers.
  • Demonstrated skills with project management tools and methodologies, software development lifecycle, and problem solving.
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects. Ability to meet stated customer initiated deadlines.
  • Demonstrated success in the leading and managing of people in a team-based environment.
  • Two to five years experience as a technical project manager or project leader in an online commerce or software venue, scrum master preferred.
  • The ability to travel up to 25%

Desired Requirements

  • Experience in Supply Chain and Business Application systems (ERP) and terminology.
  • Experience with XML, Java, UNIX, Oracle and DB2.
