Internal PC Technician

Apptricity is looking for a PC Tech guru to join our growing team. This position will provide daily support to individual employees. The PC Technician will be responsible for a range of products including maintenance for all computers, scanners, printers and software. The ideal candidate will have experience with a wide array of Microsoft Products.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Installing/supporting hardware (PC, wireless access point, phone system)
  • Installing/supporting various software (Adobe, MS Office Suite, etc.)
  • Troubleshooting a variety of computer issues
  • Setting up computer security measures
  • Offering technical support on site

Knowledge and professional skills:

  • Knowledge and familiarity with apple products
  • Networking or A+ Certification strongly preferred
  • 2+ years experience desktop support
  • Ability to be forward thinking in anticipation of new technologies and opportunities
  • Excellent written and verbal communication

Permanent Residency Required. U.S. citizenship may be required for security clearance.
