Lead Generation and Marketing Event Coordinator

At Apptricity, you will become an integral part of the expanding Marketing team. As a Lead Generation and Marketing Coordinator, you will work closely with Marketing and Sales. Your main objectives are to contact all Marketing generated leads to convert them into qualified leads to pass to Outside Sales as well as project management and coordination of all internal and external events.

Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for the management, coordination and execution of all internal and external events.
  • Initiate proposals with vendors, negotiate contracts, and manage vendors.
  • Collaborate with team to accomplish post event data analysis.
  • Must be comfortable spending majority of time on the phone working both inbound and outbound leads.
  • Conduct discovery calls to potential clients in order to asses need and schedule meeting with Outside Sales.
  • Be able to effectively and comfortably speak with prospects (mid-level managers to executives) and collect information.
  • Conduct research on potential client to better understand their organization, strategy, mission and more.
  • Ability to learn and manage data in Salesforce CRM, organize and keep track of scheduled tasks to be performed, and research companies.
  • Build and cultivate prospect relationships by initiating communications and conducting follow-up communications in order to move leads through the sales funnel.
  • Promptly follow-up with prospects by sending marketing materials, thought leadership and other relevant value add materials.
  • Work with the Sales and Marketing to develop and grow the sales pipeline.
