Sr Linux System Administrator

Apptricity Corporation is looking for a seasoned Linux administrator to join our growing team of IT professionals to lead the charge of software installations and upgrades to operating systems and layered software packages. The primary focus will be to provide a highly available, consistent, and resilient operating environment for application deployments. This includes both engineering design and support responsibilities.

The ideal candidate will have SME level Linux knowledge and experience and can work with all internal customers from the CEO to the receptionist with ease and finesse.

Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for the installation, configuration and maintenance of Linux and Solaris systems
  • Install, configure, and tune Java Application servers including: Tomcat, JBoss, WebLogic, and Websphere /li>
  • Installation and configuration of other COTS and open source software.
  • Troubleshoot Java applications on Linux, UNIX, and Windows
  • Troubleshoot system hardware provide failure analysis and recovery.
  • Troubleshoot network issues related to WAN and LAN access to Java applications and other network accessible services (SFTP, DNS, WINS, SSH, VPN, Mail, Apache)
  • Installation of database servers (Oracle, MySQL, SQL Server)
  • Operate in a production data center environment with minimal supervision
  • Interface directly and indirectly with client staff and 3rd party vendors both technical and non-technical.

Minimum Requirements:

  • 8-10 years of professional experience with Red Hat, CentOS or Fedora Linux
  • 2-4 years of experience with Solaris UNIX
  • 3-5 years of experience with Red Hat KVM or QEMU based virtual machines
  • 3-5 years of experience with kickstart/PXE boot
  • 3-5 years of experience with Java Application servers
  • 5 years of experience with Apache web server
  • 2-4 years of scripting (Perl, Bash)
  • 1-3 years Nagios or other monitoring experience

Desired Skills:

  • 3 – 5 years of Windows Server experience including domain administration
  • 1 – 3 years of experience running MySQL Clustered databases
  • Team lead experience

Permanent Residency Required. U.S. citizenship may be required for security clearance.
