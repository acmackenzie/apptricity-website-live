Presales System Engineer (SE)

The primary role of a Presales System Engineer (SE) is to support the Sales Team to complete licensed software sales. The SE provides the functional and technical expertise to help prospective clients understand how Apptricity solutions will provide value to their organizations.

Responsibilities:

  • Become a subject matter expert on Apptricity solutions across all product segments, in support of all sales cycle activities, including, but not limited to:
    • Discovery sessions
    • RFI & RFP responses
    • Trade shows
    • Solution demonstrations
    • Conference room pilots
    • Proof of concept sessions
    • On-going questions and answers
  • Work closely with prospective clients in discovery sessions and ongoing follow-up discussions to ensure clear and concise communication is taking place
  • Create and adapt presentations to address prospective clients’ specific business needs and highlight functional and technical advantages of Apptricity solutions
  • Assist in maintaining product demonstration environments and enter/configuring demonstration data and scenarios as required in sales cycles
  • Work with Product Management to support alignment with products to the market(s) based on prospects and customer feedback
  • Research and understand competitive offerings in the market and best positioning for our products

Requirements:

  • Minimum 2 years experience in a pre-sales or demo role, presenting software solutions to C-level executives, operations directors, and IT management
  • Minimum 3 years of software consulting experience performing implementation services
  • Experience working with Financial and/or Supply Chain applications; preferably with a major ERP software provider
  • Proficient technical skills necessary to install enterprise level software, adjust screens and load/manipulate complex data; experience writing SQL queries a plus
  • Strong presentation and communications skills with the ability to explain complex information to both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Effective public speaking skills with the ability to control an audience, succinctly convey necessary concepts, think on your feet and overcome objections
  • Positive attitude and outlook with the ability to successfully work independently with little supervision
  • Ability to collaboratively work with other team members to achieve desired goals and objectives both internal and external
  • Ability to thrive and contribute to a fast-paced environment that fosters change, creativity, and flexibility
  • Ability to travel periodically to visit customers/collect information
  • Bachelor's Degree in a related functional or technology field or comparable work experience

Important: Permanent Residency Required. U.S. citizenship may be required for security clearance.
