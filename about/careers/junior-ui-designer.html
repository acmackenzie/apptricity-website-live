Jr. UI Designer

Apptricity Corporation is seeking a UI Designer to join our creative team. We are looking for passionate candidates that think outside the box and are not afraid of exploring new territory in the pursuit of a better user experience.

The candidate must be able to create sophisticated wireframes from product requirements. They will work closely with Product Managers, Advanced Development, and Quality Assurance teams. The candidate must also be comfortable working with multidisciplinary teams and possess excellent communication skills, both verbal and written. All candidates must present a comprehensive portfolio in web, print, or PDF format to be considered and may be asked to complete a skills test and submit a writing sample. Candidates without portfolios will not be considered.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create elegant wireframe designs for web, mobile and tablet
  • Map end-to-end workflows
  • Present your interfaces to project teams and gather input, feedback, and even critiques from customers and make modifications and updates in a timely manner
  • Solid understanding of the principles behind user experience, web navigation, SEO, user habits, current design trends, cross-browser and platform compatibility issues
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment, while remaining flexible and adaptive to change
  • Must be able to prioritize daily, weekly, and monthly tasks and projects effectively
  • Must take direction well as this position is in the spotlight as we formulate new enterprise wide releases

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Design, Interaction Design, or related subject
  • Highly proficient with Adobe Creative Suite CS5/CS6, proficiency in Axure a plus
  • Advanced experience utilizing web scripting methods a plus

Desired Skills and Experience

  • 3+ years of experience in UI Design, software environment experience a BIG plus
  • Create engaging user interfaces for the web, mobile, and tablet
  • Experience in product design, information architecture and interaction design
  • Experience in creating a variety of design assets, including user scenarios, competitor analyses, and wireframes
  • Ability to utilize and hand code valid, semantic HTML(5), CSS and JavaScript

Permanent Residency Required. U.S. citizenship may be required for security clearance.
