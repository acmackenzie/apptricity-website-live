Project Coordinator

The primary role of the Project Coordinator (AD) is to maintain Advanced Development project plans to ensure actual work corresponds to planned work. The ideal candidate will enhance department and organization reputation by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests, while exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments. The Project Coordinator acts as a liaison between departments to ensure timeliness and accuracy throughout the project lifecycle.

Responsibilities:

  • Maintain Advanced Development project plans, including comparing MS Project baseline tasks against logged time for Jira stories and tasks, to ensure actual work corresponds to planned work.
  • Gather project details and statistics to produce the weekly Project Impact Reports, for all Advanced Development projects.
  • Keep Advance Development and others informed about project status and issues that may impact client relations
  • Enforce compliance of the Maintenance and Release Management business processes.
  • Update Agile boards daily with tickets being worked,
  • Ensure all project statuses are clearly visible to project team members
  • Conduct project standup meetings, particularly when the PM or technical lead as required
  • Act as liaison between development, Professional Services, Quality Assurance, and other departments as needed
  • Report project status to internal customers about active advanced development projects: both new base product projects and maintenance release versions efforts.
  • Conduct meetings twice a day with professional services group and QA, to resolve client issues quickly and efficiently.
  • Will act as Configuration Manager (CM) for deploying application changes to the QA and staging environments and releases new application versions to Nexus.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s Degree Computer Science, or other related subject
  • Must be knowledgeable about specific types of platform deployments, specifically, Web, iOS, Android, and Windows CE deployments.
  • Scrum Experience
  • 3-5 years of experience supporting Project Managers in the delivery of development projects
  • Proficient in MS Project a and the MS Office Suite of products
  • Knowledge of software development lifecycle
  • Effective communication skills including verbal, written and presentation skills
  • Proven ability to work effectively both independently and in a team based environment
  • Demonstrated willingness to be flexible and adaptable to changing priorities
  • Strong multi-tasking and organizational skills
  • Ability to meet stated customer initiated deadlines
