Career Opportunities

We want you to join the Apptricity Team!

Apptricity Corporation is a global technology company headquartered in Dallas, Texas with satellite offices in California, District of Columbia and London. Knowledgeable leaders partnered with passionate employees is the driving force behind our continued success. Apptricity is proudly recognized as a best-of-breed supply chain management and spend management mobile enterprise solution provider.

We have fostered an environment where creative, hardworking, goal-oriented individuals thrive. Together, we build innovative, high-quality SaaS products. Our diverse employee population embodies the sleeves-rolled-up, everyone-dig-in spirit found in successful software development companies and there are many opportunities available to become a valued member of the Apptricity Team. Come join us!

Just a few of the many perks and benefits unique to Apptricity:

Account Executive

Android Developer
Apptricity Corporation is looking for a skilled Android developer to help expand its mobile solutions and also be interested in becoming proficient in additional mobile platforms.
Database Administrator
Apptricity is looking for a talented and agile Database Administrator to help us develop, maintain and monitor databases in our heterogeneous database environment.
Graphic Design Intern
Apptricity Corporation is looking for a creative, outgoing Graphic Design intern to join our team this fall.
Human Resources Manager
Apptricity Corporation is looking for a Human Resources Manager to perform day-to-day HR/benefit operations.
Java/J2EE (Mid-level) Developer
Apptricity Corporation is adding Mid-Level Java/J2EE developers to expand our product suite. Positions include JSP front end focus as well as EJB backend developers.
Project Manager Applications
