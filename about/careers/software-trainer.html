Software Trainer

At Apptricity, we are pioneering radical shifts in the enterprise software experience. Apptricity application suites focus on finance, supply chain, and workforce management. Our solutions optimize customer resources, reduce operational costs, and can be customized to any type of business. Apptricity technology is so flexible and adaptable that it can configured, integrated, and deployed faster than any enterprise solution currently on the market.

Apptricity is searching for a superior trainer to update the training materials and teach instructor-led and web-based classes to a variety of audiences both functional and technical. The classes will be for customers as well as internal personnel and will either be held in Apptricity training room in Irving, Texas or on-site at the customer location. Successful candidate will be a self-guided learner, able to work with minimal guidance and direction to learn the software and classroom techniques, then teach each in a fun, energetic and engaging manner.

Job Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent work experience
  • Minimum of five years software training experience
  • Strong skills in PowerPoint, Word and other software delivery products
  • Effective written and verbal communications skills
  • Ability to learn new subjects quickly and transfer that knowledge clearly and effectively
  • Ability to think analytically when solving issues that arise during training
  • Ability to properly manage a classroom and keep the training on topic and on track
  • Ability to keep students engaged and interested across multiple days of training
  • Ability to effectively communicate complex business software processes
  • Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to work both in a team and independently
  • Ability to exercise patience as a trainer with trainees possessing varying degree of computer knowledge/system skills
  • Ability to manage several projects at one time with variable deadlines
  • Ability to self-manage time and work independently; deadline and results oriented
  • Up to 30% domestic travel will be required
  • Experience developing and managing WBT a definite plus

Job Responsibilities:

  • Work to become a subject matter expert (SME) by conducting thorough research and building quality relationships with internal and external SMEs
  • Integrate yourself into the product life cycles to gather knowledge throughout the process
  • Organize and lead multiple day training sessions for all the basic Apptricity software applications – both functional and administrative classes
  • Demo products over the web to familiarize customers with new product features
  • Help trouble shoot user problems and work with other SMEs to solve problems
  • Relay suggestions or needs of the users to Product Managers and help with ideas for upgrades to the software
  • Demonstrate a high degree of skill in communication and positive interaction with internal staff and customers
  • Update training materials to include changes and upgrades to software applications
  • Work with internal IT to get virtual images reset and ready for each class
  • Print, bind and organize all training and student materials and get room setup for class
  • Handles multiple tasks and responsibilities with accuracy and within time frame as assigned
  • Perform other duties as assigned
