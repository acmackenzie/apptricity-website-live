Administrative Assistant

Apptricity Corporation is looking for an Administrative Assistant to assist our Chief Technology Officer. The Assistant is responsible for professionally and efficiently managing calendars, telephone calls and messages, as well as performing a variety of clerical duties.

Responsibilities:

  • Provide general administrative support to CTO including calendar, reports and calls
  • Provide administrative support that will require the handling of confidential and sensitive information
  • Handle follow-up tasks as needed frequently interacting with people in senior leadership roles
  • Schedule internal meetings and external meetings
  • Provide ongoing communication with immediate supervisor

Minimum Requirements:

  • 3+ years of administrative assistant experience
  • Active communicator with aptitude to communicate internally with all levels of the organization and external customers and partners, both written and verbal
  • Superb computer skills including Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint and video conferencing
  • Ability to exercise good judgment and handle high stress situations with ease
  • Must be energetic and flexible bringing a high degree of commitment and positive attitude to the team
  • Highly organized and with a strong work ethic

Permanent Residency Required. U.S. citizenship may be required for security clearance.
