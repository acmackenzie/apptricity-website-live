Apptricity Recognized as a Leading Provider in PayStream Advisors Comprehensive Guide to the Most Innovative Travel Expense Management (TEM) Solutions

Mobile expense reporting works in both connected and disconnected modes

Dallas, Texas (PRWEB) January 26, 2016

Apptricity® Corporation, a leading global provider of Internet of Things (IoT) M2M mobile enterprise solutions for supply chain management(SCM) and spend management, today announced that it has been positioned by PayStream Advisors as holding a strong position in the middle to enterprise market. PayStream Advisors provides software research, consulting and education for Finance and Procurement professionals.

PayStream Advisors 2015 Travel & Expense Management Navigator, an unbiased review of benchmark automated solutions, recognized Apptricity Travel Expense Management as a leading provider in the middle market. They also recommended that large enterprise organizations consider Apptricity because they are quickly acquiring more and more enterprise clients.

The PayStream TEM Navigator scores solutions in eleven different categories. Nine of the categories are a measure of functionality, while two focus on solution breadth and customer experience. The purpose of the report is to provide a comprehensive analysis of the top TEM solutions, provide a market overview of trends, and assist in the initial selection of solutions by potential buyers.

The research identified Apptricity TEM as a solid offering ranking high in the majority of categories. PayStream acknowledged numerous strengths without finding an obvious weakness. Strengths included functionality provided to administrators and approvers once an expense report is submitted, ease for administrators to customize the solution, automated reminders for unapproved expenses and escalation procedures, and full functional mobility working in both connected and disconnected modes when there is no internet connection.

“We are honored that PayStream Advisors has recognized us as a prominent TEM provider in the middle market and recommended that global enterprise clients evaluate us,” said Tim Garcia, President and CEO of Apptricity. “This is very much in-line with how we position Apptricity, as an enterprise best buy for an automated TEM solution; while still providing value to the small and medium market with a robust, enterprise-grade solution within their reach. Our IoT M2M composite solution approach to the mobile enterprise is providing global clients with immediate value due to the speed of implementation and enterprise functionality.”

A copy of the full report can be downloaded on PayStream Advisors website.
