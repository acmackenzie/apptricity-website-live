About Apptricity

Apptricity Corporation is a leading global provider of Internet of Things (IoT) M2M mobile enterprise solutions for supply chain management (SCM) and spend management. Apptricity solutions are valued by commercial and government organizations worldwide providing bottom line results. Apptricity solutions mobilize any enterprise with unprecedented levels of real-time information and business intelligence so management has visibility into every action and transaction within the enterprise and among its partners, customers and suppliers. Powered by Apptricity JetStream platform, our solutions enhance legacy enterprise applications and extend the value of information technology investments. Our platform enables us to address the universal objectives of business, government and the military with accelerated business processes and command visibility.
