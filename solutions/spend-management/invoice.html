Start-to-Finish Automation
Invoice converts paper invoices to digital images, stores them, routes them for approvals, matches them to purchase orders (POs) and prepares them for payment — decreasing turnaround time by as much as 65 percent.
Efficient Workflow
Configurable, built-in workflow routes invoices for approval with escalation according to your timelines. Managers can approve or deny invoices or easily forward them to another approver as needed.
Mobile Access
In addition to accessing Invoice through any Web browser, Apptricity offers a specifically designed mobile application, giving managers approval ability on the go.
Business Rules
Invoice applies your organization's predefined business rules, enforcing compliance with corporate policies and freeing accounts payable (AP) personnel from the task of manual enforcement.
Permanent History
Exception and rule-based audits can occur before or after processing. The result is real-time visibility, along with a permanent record of transaction history. Electronic document retrieval eliminates time-consuming manual file searches.
Payment Notifications
Invoice forwards approved payments to your AP system. Notifications of payment are submitted to suppliers, capturing early payment discounts and reducing disputes.
Go Green
Automating the start-to-finish invoicing process makes paper trails unnecessary. Convert paper invoices to a digital format, and reduce paper even more by attaching scanned and fax-to-image documents to digital transactions.
Flexible Distribution
Invoice offers distribution at both the invoice header level and the individual line item level. Distributions can be quickly imported and adjusted on-the-fly as needed.
OCR Templates
Invoice integrates Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology, creating custom templates for your key vendors. Invoices vendors send are automatically created and filled in without manual intervention.
