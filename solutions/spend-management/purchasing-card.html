Purchasing Card

Consolidate and Automate Purchasing Card Activity.

Eliminate the need for accounts payable personnel to match dozens or hundreds of documents and approvals with each purchasing card (P-Card) statement. Apptricity Purchasing Card Management consolidates P-Card activities within a single, automated solution, maximizing the value of your corporate P-Card program. You gain total control with a distinct account listing, start-to-finish oversight and the ability to configure rules according to your business needs. This vendor-neutral solution supports all card carriers and types, including travel cards, custodian cards, ghost cards and fleet cards.

Take advantage of our comprehensive P-Card management capabilities, conveniently accessible from a web browser or mobile device:

  • Provision cards according to title, limit or geographical location.
  • Use predetermined workflows to route reports and ensure fast approvals.
  • Free valuable accounting personnel from tedious manual tasks so they can focus on strategic business goals.
  • Meet corporate and regulatory compliance demands.
  • Automate the import of purchase data to help eliminate data entry errors.
