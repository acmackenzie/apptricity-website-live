Spend Management Overview

Streamline and Automate Spend Management, Reporting and Compliance
while Improving Enterprise-Wide Visibility

Gain enterprise-wide visibility with the Apptricity Spend Management solution suite, a group of integrated mobile solutions that brings efficiency to your organization's expense, purchasing, time and attendance and reporting operations. Time-saving and automated, our solutions streamline core functions, eliminating many cumbersome manual processes. You save money and multiply efficiencies while establishing a powerful competitive edge.

This innovative platform is uniquely designed to increase your productivity with a variety of solutions that address spend management. Our customizable solutions provide full visibility and compliance with federal regulatory standards, including the Family Medical Leave Act (FMLA), Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA), Physician Payment Sunshine Act (PPSA) and Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX).

A complete line of purchase management solutions is available to reduce operational costs and improve visibility and productivity. You can automate functions such as purchasing, invoicing and transaction processing while centralizing all procurement activities.

Operating system (OS) agnostic, all Apptricity solutions are powered by Apptricity JetStream platform and utilize a common service-oriented architecture (SOA) platform, enabling you to select individual modules for the integration of critical finance-related functions. Adding solutions from Apptricity's Supply Chain Management solution suite enables you to tie in supply chain operations and automate your entire enterprise.
