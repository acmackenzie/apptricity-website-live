Gain Complete Control with Integrated Purchasing and Invoicing

Why waste time and money due to procurement inefficiencies? By automating procurement with Apptricity Procure-to-Pay, you gain complete control of your procurement stream and payments. Our fully integrated procure-to-pay (P2P or purchase-to-pay) solution includes automated purchasing and invoicing to support a smooth, efficient process from start to finish. It begins with goods and services requisitioning and ends with automated payment integration into your accounts payable (AP) or enterprise resource planning (ERP) system. If needed, optional warehouse management capabilities enable you to include fulfillment as well.

Why Apptricity Procure-to-Pay?

More visibility, more savings. P2P automation saves you money! Improved insight and control over purchasing have a clear impact on your bottom line. To maximize contract benefits and prevent vendors from over charging, use Procure-to-Pay to understand your spend and help ensure contract compliance.

Improved accuracy. Manual processing can be error-prone and cumbersome. With Procure-to-Pay, automation replaces manual efforts and inefficient systems for time savings, high visibility and complete control. Finance leaders increase the accuracy of their financial statements and forecasts, and operational leaders improve their staff’s vendor interactions.

Increased value from purchases. Armed with reports, analyses and key performance indicators (KPIs), you can more easily maximize your sourcing negotiations’ value and return on investment (ROI). Our Buyer Portal ensures automated and systematic purchases from only contracted vendors and supports negotiated discounts.

Reduced costs and risks. Greater procurement efficiency drives your overall costs down. You minimize the risks of vendor overcharge, maverick buying from non-contract vendors and losing discounts.

Efficient collaboration. Extending your P2P process via our Supplier Portal enables suppliers to connect and collaborate with you more effectively and efficiently.

Seamless user experience. Built on a common service-oriented architecture (SOA), Procure-to-Pay provides a seamless user experience throughout the P2P cycle – from requisition to purchase order (PO), PO to invoice and invoice to reimbursement.

Fast launch, low maintenance. Procure-to-Pay is offered as a subscription-based service. With nothing to install and automatic updates, the need to rely on information technology (IT) resources diminishes sharply. If you're transitioning from a system with little to no automation, you'll like the fast ROI.

Easy integration. The solution integrates easily with AP and ERP systems, improving speed, usability and performance.

Scalability. Our modular design enables you to select only the pieces of the procurement solution that your organization needs. The solution expands as you gain functionality.
