Mobile Enterprise Solutions

Anytime. Anywhere. Everywhere

Today's enterprise is pressured to drive value by becoming increasingly agile and leveraging mobility to stay competitive. Apptricity has responded to this trend with the Enterprise Mobility suite, a secure mobile system powered by Apptricity JetStream platform.

The Enterprise Mobility suite provides answers to the complex geographical problems of a disparate workforce, increasing volumes of critical assets, extended supply chain complexities and real-time in-motion management. The suite adds significant value by supplying true visibility and reporting for assets, workflows and processes across the entire organization, regardless of ownership.

Respond Quickly and Effectively

Apptricity's Enterprise Mobility suite is agile and well equipped to keep ahead of the demands of today's enterprises and enable them to respond dynamically to real-time operational situations and market influences. Flexible deployment options suit both perpetual licensing and Software as a Service (SaaS) environments.

Increase Competitive Advantage

The suite empowers organizations to increase their competitive advantage by strengthening supply chain collaboration, boosting productivity and improving enterprise-wide efficiencies.

Enterprises can now expand their capabilities in critical ways:

  • Report on previously unattainable metrics
  • Gain vital business intelligence regarding mobile workforce performance
  • Leverage easily justified and cost-effective tactical enablement that grows into strategic solutions
  • Reduce timescales to realize net returns through rapid deployment and user adoption
  • Leverage existing infrastructure and resources to minimize costs
  • Decrease operational costs through shared resources and improved workflows
  • Reduce exposure to the risk of time-sensitive contract breaches as well as compliance and audit failures
  • Enhance health and safety programs and reduce insurance claims and premiums
  • Improve disconnects between suppliers and consumers

Extend Automation beyond the Desktop

The Enterprise Mobility suite enables organizations to extend Apptricity's powerful automation solutions beyond the desktop. Users can track asset locations, manage work orders, create and submit expense reports and more, all from their smartphones or tablets. Mobile integration increases connectivity and usability, providing critical capabilities such as the following:

  • Agile delivery
  • Large-scale logistics
  • Field services support
  • Critical response support
  • Perishable goods management
  • Remote asset management
  • Consumer service enhancements
  • Improved enterprise collaboration
